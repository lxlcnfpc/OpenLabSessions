Introduction

The CNFPC Makerspace offers Open Lab Sessions every Monday from 16:05 until 18:00. During this time, the Makerspace opens its doors for everyone at the CNFPC (students and staff). Visit us to use our machinery and work on your personal projects, to learn something new in one of our presentations or to just have a chat and meet new people.

Our Offer

  • Tools and Machinery

    One of our main goals is to allow everyone at the CNFPC the use of our sophisticated tools and machines.

Participate

If you want to show your project on this site, suggest your own event for the makerspace or need our help with a hard problem, feel free to reach out to us via email, or just visit us at our Open Lab Sessions or one of our events.

Moreover, if you want to participate in a project, but don't know, where to start, we always propose a series of things you can work on and ongoing projects which you can join.

About this site

This website serves as a hub for our makerspace community, featuring event dates, project showcases, and educational resources. Please note that our open lab sessions are currently restricted to employees of the CNFPC and students enrolled in courses at our institution.
